I started with LMP in 1999 and Brother Abel joined us in 2001. Both of us are a product of the 1000 Missionary Movement, a mission training school here in the Philippines. ... Rigth now, we have the privilege to preach the Word of God inside the maximum security cell to lifers.
Louie Benitez, Prison Ministry Coordinator (left in the picture)
We conduct bible studies every day in the four prison facilities of Sablayan Prison and Penal Farm, and additionaly weekly church services on Saturday. At the present we have our own church in each facility, administering 260 baptized inmates.

Some testimonies of inmate brethren
